Game Log

Tobin is at the Alpaca Farm with Durvish (aka Princess Layla) and Morthos.

An old woman (Kaylee) invites them into the house. She delegates tasks: Morthos immediately starts chopping broccoli. Tobin rolls to see how well he can cook: 10 - the meat is in a probably edible form.

Princess Layla and Kaylee are catching up. Tobin asks what he's doing here.

Kaylee says they're pretty far northwest of the city. She just has this Alpaca Farm. It's away from the city so it's pretty safe. Kaylee was one of Princess Layla's wizarding teachers , but she's retired now. Princess Layla has been coming out here secretly, and pretending to be a bad student.

Flashback: Princess Layla is on a boat and falls into the water. Kaylee casts waterwalk and gets Princess Layla, but she's seen. Another teacher sees this and notices that – water walk is not a wizard move, it's only for sorcerors. So she was kicked out of the wizarding school, so now she's on this farm.

Kaylee asks Tobin about what happened to Ainsley because she was on a mission to find the processor. Kaylee offers Tobin a chance to send a message to Ainsley. The message is: "Durvish = Layla? Tykolos+Sirrus = Comedy? Where are you? Did you find procesor?"

Ainsley (in the The Feywild) gets the message. Trying to guess how to respond. "Found Gwidian. Found processor. In Feywild, blue rose farm. En route to FUCC HQ. Help!"

Kaylee and Princess Layla agree to send some people to The Feywild to rescue Ainsley. The fine print on planeshift requires some specific things. A forked metal rod worth at least 250g that's attuned to The Feywild. Kaylee doesn't have one and doesn't know where to find one. Although she is a sorceror, and she can do "metamagic" and distort one attribute of a spell. But she would need something of equivalent magical power. She proposes that the group help her with some chores, and tomorrow she can planeshift to The Feywild. Morthos is psyched to do some chores. Tobin hates chores and ponders how he can manipulate Morthos into doing his chores for him.

Tobin gets up at 4am to do chores. Lots of Animal handling.

  • First chore is to sheer an Alpaca. Tobin rolls somewhat poorly. Morthos sheers enough wool to make a pair of socks - warm-weather clothing.
  • Second chore is to milk an alpaca. Tobin rolls well. Get's a glass of milk (equivalent of potion of healing). Morthos also gets a glass of milk.
  • Third chore is to bury a deceased alpaca. Tobin recommends that we dispose of the corpse in a funeral pyre. Kaylee convinces him to try the burial anyway to plant a seed. Tobin finds an excellent burial site.

(The party is distracted about who did the voice for Kuzco in emporer's new groove – it's David Spade)

  • Morthos is digging the hole. He does a good job. He plants the seed and the seed instantly pops up into a sprout. The sprout generates a small apple. It's a Circle of life apple. When eaten, the eater cannot go below 1HP for 1 minute.
  • Chore 4: Alpaca mating. Feed the alpacas a moonbloom herb to promote mating. Tobin rolls 10. Morthos finds a frisky male alpaca. Kaylee hands Tobin an amulet of detect magic to see if there's some "magic" happening in the alpaca sex shed. Tobin plays a joke on Morthos - tells him to check on the alpacas. Morthos doesn't fall for it.

Tobin notices two large Dire wolves lurking in the distance. They give off a hormone that is detectable by preditors from a long way away. The dire wolves are approaching to prey on the alpacas. Tobin does an eldritch blast immediately. It's battle time.

Morthos is going to deliver an alpaca baby. Princess Layla is going to join the fight against the dire wolves to stop them from eating the alpaca baby.

(editor's note: the stakes of this fight with the dire wolves is unclear. I'm frankly a little surprised that Tobin is making so much of an effort to ensure the successful delivery of this alpaca baby)

Tobin is taking massive damage from these Dire Wolves.

The alpaca has successfully given birth. One wolf was killed, and the other ran away.

Kaylee welcomes Tobin and Princess Layla back into her house. Kaylee can do a planeshift using the placenta of the newly birthed alpaca, because it's really magical.

Kaylee adds that she used to be an adventurer, and she's been to The Feywild. She has an ally there – the arch druid of the stars. She doesn't recall any blue rose farms. The Arch Druid may be convinced to help Tobin when presented with the Fallen Star Necklace.

Meanwhile, Tykolos is in the servant's quarters (long rest). Jenkins informs me that Vanalos will be here soon, and leads me to a side room. He should be here shortly. (7 1/2 hours)

In walks a satyr. Vanalos. Tykolos shares the details of his map

Vanalos shares that he's made two deals with two devils. He comments on the horn fragment that I collected. He's been coming and going to The Feywild a lot. He's been going thisways and thatways a lot. He asks if we were successful. I share that Ainsley has the Spirit of Mistlicah, and describe her phsysical appearance.

Vanalos shares his story. We were in the forest playing music. There was a spooky cave and Tykolos was the smart one and said lets go home. Vanalos decided to go back to the cave to see what's inside, just to prove to himselve that he could be brave. He shouldn't have gone on his own, but he did.

I see that Vanalos is wearing a necklace with a black gem. It sounded like a child crying, and he wanted to protect the child. So Vanalos put the necklace and was cursed – by Theresdun. Apparently wearing the necklace binds him in a warlock deal. He has warlock powers, but he's bound to service to Theresdun. Vanalos is now charged with destroying anything hold.

Vanalos was involved in the attack on the caves of Mistlicah. He ran into Sirrus and formed a bond with him. Sirrus is going to find a way to get to the Spirit of Mistlicah. Sirrus doesn't know Vanalos works for Theresdun. Can we kill Theresdun?

Vanalos says he wants to catch up to the The Feywild and get the Spirit of Mistlicah from Ainsley.

Jenkins has been listening at the door. Vanalos needs to kill this guy to keep the secret – that he's working for Theresdun – from Sirrus. Vanalos kills Jenkins with ease.

Vanalos leads Tykolos to a portal to the The Feywild. We can step into the magic mirror.

We try to talk our way through the castle to the castle. Vanalos has blood on his hands, and the guards are suspicious.

We go through the mirror-portal and we arrive in The Feywild, specifically at FUCC HQ. There's a bunch of mirror portals, a bunch of wardrobes with coats in them, a pharmacy with red pills. Enchanted lightposts. A jewely case with magic rings. Lots of shoes (like ruby shoes). Lots of rabbit holes. There's even a london tube train platform sign. (Lots of pop-culture reference to shifting to another plane). Lots of people taking blood samples back and forth.

Vanalos is playing it cool, he's the leader of FUCC, after all. Tykolos plays it cool and follows Vanalos through the HQ.

Meanwhile, Ainsley and Shubby are en route to FUCC HQ.

Ainsley communicates with Shubby. To develop a hit-and-run operation. Shubby will kick one of the dryads in the shins and run away to create a diversion. All four dryads chase after Shubby, clearing the way for Ainsley, Suet, and Gwidian. They are continuing on their way to the castle.

They reach the castle. Shubby is shaking the dryads.

The castle is described. Inside the castle is a statue garden, with lots of history. The castle is circular shaped. Each of the 12 circles has a little zoo/garden area. There are 12 different circles:

1- Circle of Stars
2- Circle of the Moon
3- Circle of Wildfire
4- Circle of Spores
5- Circle of the Arctic
6- Circle of the Coast
7- Circle of the Desert
8- Circle of the Forest
9- Circle of the Grassland
10- Circle of the Mountain
11- Circle of the Swamp
12- Circle of the Underdark

Ainsley goes to the circle of the moon. We hear all about the plants and animals in this circle.

Ainsley investigates the circle of stars now. The arch druid is Celeste Dawnwhisper. We hear all about the animals and plans of this circle. There's an area of the garden that seems to be missing: the Nebula Orchid.

Ainsley investigates the circle of the Arctic now. The arch druid is Glacia. We learn all about the animals and plants of this circle.

Shubby catches up with the group. Shubby wants to get the attention of one of the arch-druids. The person we need to talk to is a minotaur: Sagetorn Ledgerhorn. He's like the museum administrator. He can help us setup and appointment with one of the arch-druids.

Ainsley, Gwidian, Suet, and Shubby are at the castle to setup a meeting with an arch-druid.

Tykolos is at the FUCC HQ.

Tobin is in The Feywild, but at an unknown location within.
