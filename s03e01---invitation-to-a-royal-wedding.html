Recap

Synopsis

The party arrives on a landing at the foot of a waterfall.

Suet introduces herself to Giordano who's travelling through the area. Giordano was evidently charmed by a suspicious dryad and gave away his Blue Rose Powder to it. Giorano also reveals that his blue rose powder comes from a supplier in the Hawkwind Islands. This person allegedly has a wardrobe that transports you to The Feywild.

Morthos has intel regarding the imminent poisoning of the Princess Layla – Sirrus is allegedly going to be the one poisoning her. He leads us to the Drunken Druid to deliver the message. Suet disguises herself.

At the Drunken Druid we are introduced to Durvish, who is sitting in a corner inside a Zone of Truth. Durvish briefs the party on Operation Autumn Circle, a mission to capture the processor from Sirrus at the Royal Wedding. The party will use a wand of reducing to shrink the processor and fit it into a bag of holding. The party was given a forged invitation addressed to Lowel Brassborn and Lorna Brassborn, two fake identities claiming to be a part of the Brassborn family. The party also gets a key to the storeroom and hand-drawn map of the castle.

Suet gets discovered and Durvish flees. Suet has been accused of killing Gwidian with a poisoned love potion. Suet denies this.

The party prepares for the Royal Wedding:

  • Tykolos will bluff his way in as a zookeeper.
  • Ainsley will disguise as Lorna Brassborn
  • Suet will disguise as Lowel Brassborn
  • Tobin will go as an Arcane games employee
  • Quonk will go as a bowling pin setter
