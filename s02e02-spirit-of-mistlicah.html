S02E02 - Spirit of Mistlicah

Synopsis

The party continued into the cave, with Morthos and Lamberton still bound (both arguing over who should be killed). The party used their three keys in a door puzzle with colored keyholes.

While most of the party ventured into the cavern of colored walls, Tobin stayed behind to keep an eye on our bound friends, Morthos and Lamberton.

Tykolos, Quonk, Ainsley, and a cohort of NPC characters were able to use the keys to move from room to room, until we reached a pair of lizards who attacked us on sight. The party made quick work of them and pressed on.

Meanwhile, Tobin detected the sound of an incoming spider, and Quonk chose to turn back to provide aid. Unfortunately, the spider reached Tobin before Quonk arrived, and paralyzed him. Quonk finally caught up with the spider and killed it, but Lamberton was able to slip his binds and climb out of the cave in the commotion. Morthos attempted to pursue Lamberton, but couldn’t climb the rope out of the cave with his wrists still bound.

As Quonk fought off the spider, Tykolos, Ainsley, and the rest of the NPCs arrived in a candlelit room with a colored wheel in the center. On top of the wheel was a page torn from a book that reads (in cat-person language)

The spirit can only be obtained by one who wants to use it to protect, not for power.

We quickly learned that turning the wheel caused the keyholes to change color. This would allow us to use the blue key and go deeper into the cave, but it also released a swarm of bats and exposed a pit which the party would need to cross over to get back on track.

Tykolos attempted to cross the pit, but lost his footing. Quonk, having negotiated a deal with Morthos, had come back to the front of the pit. He used his climbing rope to tie a lasso and loop it around Tykolos's horn to pull him up.

Ainsley was the next to attempt the crossing, and she also slipped. Quonk managed to pull her to safety as well, with Tykolos shooting at the swarm of bats attacking the rest of the party.

The NPCs that were with us managed to kill off the remaining bats and together we managed to get back to the entry point to proceed through the newly opened passaged way downward to the sound of a waterfall...
