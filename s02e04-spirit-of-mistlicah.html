S02E04 - Spirit of Mistlicah

Gameplay

No obvious ways in or out of the city other than the way we came in

We go to the community college library. Is there a reference book we can use to understand what the Spirit of Mistlicah is all about?

Are the books intact? Present day, the books are burnt, but we can use the magical blue light to see what the book looked like in the past.

Ainsley rolls investigation, and finds a book on this subject. In cat-person language

Echo is going to read the book.

Sochani has some background on the Spirit of Mistlicah

4:54 PM while in the library, we hear a sound from the staircase. There's also an industrial building with mine carts going in and out.

4:57 PM Tykolos notices a satyr in the memory. It's my brother. His horn got sliced off. He slumps the ground next to the horn. No skeleton of the satyr. I pick up the satyr horn and put it in my bag.

5:02 PM Lamberton is at th stairs. He rigged the stairs to explode in 2 minutes. If we try to fight him, the explosives will go off unless we toss him the Spirit of Mistlicah.

5:04 PM Lamberton is knocked out by a lion paw. It's a new character, a lion-person. He snags the detonator. His name is Charlemane.

5:06 PM Sirrus steps out from behind with several guards! He demands the Spirit of Mistlicah

5:07 PM Sirrus plans to kill the King's heir so the king will have to name him as the new successor.

5:17 PM Tykolos tries to ram Charlemane to drop the timer (fails)

5:17 PM Tobin tries to dive on top of the timer

5:18 PM Tobin explains to Charlemane that smashing the timer will detonate it.

5:20 PM Tobin adds 45 minutes to the timer to avoid detonation.

5:52 PM Charlemane wants the Spirit of Mistlicah. He tries to intimidate Sochani, who has the necklace. Sochani doesn't give it up.

5:53 PM Tykolos threatens Charlemane. Charlemane attacks!
