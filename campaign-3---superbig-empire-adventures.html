S03E01 - Invitation to a Royal Wedding

Synopsis

Synopsis

The party arrives on a landing at the foot of a waterfall.

Suet introduces herself to Giordano who's travelling through the area. Giordano was evidently charmed by a suspicious dryad and gave away his Blue Rose Powder to it. Giorano also reveals that his blue rose powder comes from a supplier in the Hawkwind Islands. This person allegedly has a wardrobe that transports you to The Feywild.

Morthos has intel regarding the imminent poisoning of the Princess Layla – Sirrus is allegedly going to be the one poisoning her. He leads us to the Drunken Druid to deliver the message. Suet disguises herself.

At the Drunken Druid we are introduced to Durvish, who is sitting in a corner inside a Zone of Truth. Durvish briefs the party on Operation Autumn Circle, a mission to capture the processor from Sirrus at the Royal Wedding. The party will use a wand of reducing to shrink the processor and fit it into a bag of holding. The party was given a forged invitation addressed to Lowel Brassborn and Lorna Brassborn, two fake identities claiming to be a part of the Brassborn family. The party also gets a key to the storeroom and hand-drawn map of the castle.

Suet gets discovered and Durvish flees. Suet has been accused of killing Gwidian with a poisoned love potion. Suet denies this.

The party prepares for the Royal Wedding:

  • Tykolos will bluff his way in as a zookeeper.
  • Ainsley will disguise as Lorna Brassborn
  • Suet will disguise as Lowel Brassborn
  • Tobin will go as an Arcane games employee
  • Quonk will go as a bowling pin setter

S03E02 - Operation Autumn Circle

Synopsis

Synopsis

The party departs for the Hawkwind Islands for the Royal Wedding

Tobin departs as an employee of the Arcane Games, and meets Vivaldi, a drummer for the band The Honey Badgers. Tykolos departs on a boat next to him, and summons a Giant Badger (named Bandit) to use as part of his zookeeper disguise. Ainsley (disuised as Lorna Brassborn) and Suet (disguised as Lowel Brassborn) develop their backstory.

The party arrives at a security checkpoint and has to hand over their weapons. Tykolos successfully convinces the guard to allow him to keep his quarterstaff under the explanation that it's a zookeeper's tool.

The party enters the Royal Wedding. Sirrus gives a speach about how this wedding is about forming an alliance to defeat Theresdun and the Dunheads (a lie? – we know that Sirrus intends to poison the princess at this wedding). Then Sirrus gives a – surprisingly excellent – comedy routine to the crowd.

Suet and Ainsley stumble upon Morthos in the kitchen. Morthos recognizes them immediately, and shares his plan to hide in the laundry room and disguise as one of Sirrus's guards to acquire a key to the room with the processor in it.

Tykolos, Suet, and Ainsley form a plan to cause a distraction and run to the storeroom in the chaos. Suet and Ainsley will hide in the bag of holding until we arrive in the storeroom. However, Ainsley's step-mom Camilla notices her and starts calling. Tykolos draws a Bandit Captain and Three Guards from his deck of illusions, successfully frightening the zoo animals and causing a commotion. Tykolos runs to the storeroom carrying the bag of holding containing Suet and Ainsley.

Tykolos arrives in the storeroom and releases Suet and Ainsley. We explore the room. Suet and Ainsly follow a citrus smell to the back of the storeroom, and Tykolos hangs back. Suet and Ainsley stumble upon a strange device that resembles the Flux Capacitor. This must be the processor they were looking for. The device starts making noise and engulfs Ainsley and Suet. Tykolos is left alone in the storeroom, and is discovered by a guard.

S03E03 - Manticore Attack

Synopsis

Synopsis

Ainsley and Suet (not actively playing this session) appear inside a room within a mysterious colorful realm. They don't know it yet, but this is The Feywild. After emerging from the room, they see a suspicious dryad who is arguing with a furbolg named Shubby over a literal bag of shit. The dryad attempts to charm Ainsley, but fails. Shubby kicks the dryad and scurries away with his bag of shit while the dryad chases after him.

Meanwhile, Tykolos is escorted to a security office by three guards. Before being locked in the cell, Tykolos observes some things about the locking mechanism used by the guards. Also in the cell is Morthos and Zinfendel. Tykolos and Zinfendel collaborate on the locking mechanism and eventually escape the cell.

Meanwhile, Princess Layla entertains herself with some dwarven kong and reveals to Tobin that she got Suet's note, and if her love potion was mixed with Blue Rose Powder, it could have been screwed up, of even become poisonous. Then, a group of manticores suddenly attack the Royal Wedding. The princess flees for the photo booth with her groom, and Tobin follows along. Shortly after, Durvish emerges from the photo booth. Princess Layla and Durvish are perhaps the same person.

Amidst the chaos of the manticore attack, Tykolos and Zinfendel escape their cell, kill the guard in the office, and make their wait to Sirrus's office. Tykolos is searching for clues to find Ainsley and Suet, and Zinfendel is only interested in alcohol.

In Sirrus's office, Tykolos sees a contraption on the desk, and it appears that Sirrus uses this contraption to process his fluids using the processor which he later drinks. He also has files on Durvish and Gwidian – notably, the notes on Gwidian indicate that he was seen after his alleged death. In addition, Tykolos notices a number of files and correspondences with his brother Vanalos with the FUCC letterhead. Vanalos is evidently the head of the FUCC organization and he reports to Sirrus.

S03E04 - What the FUCC?

Synopsis

Synopsis

Ainsley and Suet follow the orange-brick road in the feywild and encounter a man standing in front of a house, talking to Shubby. After some conversation, the man reveals himself to be Gwidian, the person Suet is accused of poisoning. Gwidian reveals that Sirrus frequently sends employees to The Feywild with his fluids so he can live forever.

Shubby shares that the arch-druids have outsourced all their decision making to FUCC, the Fey Undying Cultivation Corporation. FUCC is the real processor, and is turning The Feywild into a Blue Rose Powder farm. Shubby would like nothing more than to have the arch-druids turn against FUCC and drive them out of the the feywild.

As they continue their journey, they encounter a satyr with one horn (presumably Vanalos, but they wouldn't know that). The satyr leads them to FUCC Headquarters, which Gwidian would like to destroy.

Meanwhile, the manticores attacking the Royal Wedding have already killed the queen of the Hawkwind Islands and many others. Tykolos joins the battle after reclaiming the confiscated weapons stored in the security office, and pickpockets a key of unknown purpose from Sirrus on his way to join Tobin.

During a break in the battle, Tykolos convinces Sirrus to do business with him in an effort to get closer to reuniting with his lost brother Vanalos. Sirrus agrees, and asks Tykolos to wait in the servant's quarters until Vanalos returns from his business trip.

Tobin, Durvish, and Morthos are teleported to safety, an alpaca farm owned by a woman named Kaylee.
